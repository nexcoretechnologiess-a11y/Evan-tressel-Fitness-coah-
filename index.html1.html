— FAQ

GOT QUESTIONS?

Everything you're wondering, answered upfront.

Do I have to give up my favourite foods? +
Absolutely not. The whole point is that you can eat foods you enjoy. We use flexible dieting — you hit your numbers and eat what fits. No food is off limits.
I work long hours. Is this realistic for me? +
This was literally built for people with 9-5 jobs. Workouts are 3-4 days, 45-60 minutes. The nutrition system is designed to be minimal effort with maximum output.
How fast will I see results? +
Most clients see visible change within 3-4 weeks. Significant transformation (20+ lbs) typically happens in the 8-16 week range depending on consistency.
What does the weekly check-in look like? +
You send a quick check-in form with your weight, energy levels, and photos. Evan reviews your data and sends back a feedback update and any plan adjustments within 24 hours.
How do I sign up? +
DM Evan on Instagram the word "FIT" and he'll reach out personally to see if you're a good fit for the program. He only works with clients he's confident he can genuinely help.
